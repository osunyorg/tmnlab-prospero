Anthony is a strategic and digital designer. Co-founder of Collectif Bam (external link) in 2013, and later of the cooperative Praticable (external link), he has been working independently since 2023 on projects of public interest, while also teaching his practice in various schools and universities. His work combines strategic thinking with the design of digital interfaces, visual identities, and tangible objects, with a focus on projects that are useful, open, and carefully crafted.

He mainly works with non-profit organizations, public institutions, research labs, and cultural or educational organizations. His projects address digital, societal, and environmental transitions, including generative artificial intelligence, digital commons, privacy, digital mediation, public action, and ecological, energy, and democratic issues.
