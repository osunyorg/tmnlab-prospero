Ce petit guide gratuit et entièrement consacré à vos démarches de fidélisation et la manière dont vous pouvez utiliser les données de vos publics à cette fin est un parfait premier pas pour concevoir sa stratégie. Thrive est une agence chargée de soutenir de développement des acteurs culturels en Irelande du Nord à travers la publication de ressources librement accessibles, d'études de terrain et de programmes d'accompagnement.

Quelques ressources sélectionnées
Let’s talk Loyalty Toolkit - by thrive
In this toolkit, thrive, NI's audience development agency, looks at how to evaluate loyalty, gather data on your audience’s loyalty and rate your own loyalty score.
https://www.culturehive.co.uk/resources/lets-talk-loyalty-toolkit/

Improve your Loyalty score : The Loyalty Spider Chart
This spider has only 6 legs and not 8. Each of its legs refers to all the ingredients you need for the ultimate loyalty score. Rate yourself from 1 to 10 for each item. 1 means you’re not doing so well and should be placed at the centre of the spider; 10 means you’re excelling and should be placed at the tip of the leg.
https://s3-eu-west-1.amazonaws.com/wewillthrive/Thrive-Loyalty-Spider-Chart.pdf

Organic Influencer Marketing Toolkit
In this toolkit, thrive provide a step-by-step guide for how to work with influencers to help you meet your short and long-term goals. They're focusing on organic influencers, which means this is a low-cost marketing strategy which will hopefully help you make a big impact.
https://wewillthrive.co.uk/resources/toolkits-templates/organic-influencer-marketing-toolkit
